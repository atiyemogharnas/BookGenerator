Title: The Blind Owl
Owner: Sadegh Hedayat
Creation Date: Thu Jan 01 00:00:00 GMT+03:30 1936

Content:
The Blind Owl is Sadegh Hedayat's magnum opus and a major literary work of 20th-century Iran. Written in Persian, it is narrated by an unnamed pen case painter, who addresses his murderous confessions to a shadow on his wall that resembles an owl
